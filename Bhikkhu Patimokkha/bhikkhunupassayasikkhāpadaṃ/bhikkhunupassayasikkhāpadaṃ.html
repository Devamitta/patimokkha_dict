PA 23 bhikkhunupassayasikkhāpadaṃ

yo pana bhikkhu bhikkhun'upassayaṃ upasaṅkamitvā bhikkhuniyo ovadeyya aññatra samayā, pācittiyaṃ. tatth'āyaṃ samayo, gilānā hoti bhikkhunī, ayaṃ tattha samayo.
