PA 33 paramparabhojanasikkhāpadaṃ

paramparabhojane aññatra samayā pācittiyaṃ. tatth'āyaṃ samayo, gilānasamayo, cīvaradānasamayo, cīvarakārasamayo, ayaṃ tattha samayo.
